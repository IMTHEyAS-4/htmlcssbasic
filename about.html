ABOUT THE INSTITUTE

IMTHI College of Engineering was established in 2003 with the mission to foster academic excellence and nurture technically competent professionals. Located in a serene and accessible area, the institution blends tradition and innovation to build future-ready engineers.

Spread across a 50-acre eco-friendly campus, IMTHI offers smart classrooms, modern laboratories, and research centers dedicated to AI, Robotics, and Renewable Energy. The institution is affiliated to Anna University and is recognized by AICTE, delivering education that balances academic rigor and ethical responsibility.

With a dynamic student body and passionate faculty, IMTHI cultivates creativity through events like ImthiFest and TechPulse. Whether it's coding, building, designing, or leading, students here are challenged to rise and thrive in real-world scenarios.
