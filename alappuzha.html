ALAPPPUZHA

Experience Alappuzha – The Venice of the East

Welcome to Alappuzha, the heart of Kerala’s backwater beauty. Often called the “Venice of the East,” Alappuzha is a mesmerizing destination known for its scenic houseboat cruises, lush paddy fields, and tranquil canals. Sail through the enchanting backwaters on a traditional kettuvallam (houseboat), watch the sun set over the Alappuzha Beach, and witness the thrill of the famous Nehru Trophy Boat Race. With its charming lagoons, coir villages, and Ayurvedic heritage, Alappuzha offers a unique blend of natural serenity and cultural richness. From romantic escapes to family adventures, Alappuzha welcomes every traveler with warm hospitality and unforgettable experiences. Come, drift into a world of water, wonder, and wellness in God’s Own Country.
