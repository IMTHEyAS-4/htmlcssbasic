THIRUVANANTHAPURAM

Discover Thiruvananthapuram – Where Heritage Meets Serenity

Welcome to Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of Kerala, where timeless tradition blends with coastal charm. Known for its majestic temples, pristine beaches, rich culture, and vibrant festivals, Thiruvananthapuram is a traveler’s delight nestled on the southwestern tip of India. Explore the architectural grandeur of the Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple, unwind at the golden sands of Kovalam Beach, or delve into art and history at the Napier Museum and Kuthiramalika Palace. Nature lovers can escape to the serene beauty of Ponmudi Hills or take a peaceful backwater cruise in Poovar. Whether you're a history buff, a nature enthusiast, a spiritual seeker, or simply looking for a relaxing getaway, Thiruvananthapuram offers unforgettable experiences for every kind of traveler. Plan your journey today and experience the soul of Kerala in Thiruvananthapuram – a city of culture, spirituality, and natural splendor.
