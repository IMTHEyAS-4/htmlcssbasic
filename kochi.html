Discover Kochi – The Cultural Gateway of Kerala

Welcome to Kochi, a vibrant coastal city where centuries of history meet modern charm. Known as the Queen of the Arabian Sea, Kochi is a cultural melting pot shaped by Arab, Dutch, Portuguese, and British influences — all woven into the fabric of this enchanting port city. Wander through the charming lanes of Fort Kochi, marvel at the iconic Chinese fishing nets, and explore the heritage-rich Jew Town and Paradesi Synagogue. From the colonial-era architecture and art cafés to the colorful spice markets and lively waterfront, Kochi offers a perfect blend of tradition and trend. Whether you're catching a traditional Kathakali performance, exploring the modern art scene at the Kochi-Muziris Biennale, or enjoying a peaceful sunset ferry ride, Kochi invites you to experience Kerala’s past and present in one unforgettable journey.
