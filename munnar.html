MUNNAR

Explore Munnar – The Emerald Queen of the Western Ghats

Welcome to Munnar, Kerala’s most enchanting hill station, where rolling tea gardens, misty mountains, and cool breezes create the perfect escape. Perched at 1,600 meters above sea level, Munnar is a paradise for nature lovers, honeymooners, and adventure seekers alike. Stroll through endless tea plantations, trek to the heights of Anamudi Peak, or lose yourself in the wild beauty of Eravikulam National Park, home to the rare Nilgiri Tahr. With cascading waterfalls, serene lakes like Mattupetty, and fragrant spice gardens, Munnar offers a refreshing retreat into nature’s embrace. Whether you're sipping fresh-brewed tea, hiking scenic trails, or simply soaking in the mountain views, Munnar promises peace, romance, and unforgettable memories in every moment.
