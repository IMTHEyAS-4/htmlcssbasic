ABOUT THE INSTITUTE

Founded in the year 2003, IMTHI College of Engineering (ICE) was born out of a bold dream — to build a cradle of innovation where technology meets imagination. From a modest start with just three departments and a handful of faculty, ICE has transformed into a **thriving 50-acre smart campus**, echoing with the footsteps of over 3,000 students today.

With over 10,000 alumni shaping industries and ideas across the globe, ICE remains true to its founding mantra: "Invent. Inspire. Impact." Whether you're an aspiring engineer, a curious creator, or a dreamer of a better world — there's a place for you here.
